FAQ

Q: Is it more expensive to use mechanical animals over real ones?

A: In some ways, it can be, but overall, we take out costs that would've been needed to feed, take care of, and train real living animals.
On top of that, we find it much more ethical to use robots, instead.

Q: How safe is it to attend this traveling circus?

A: It is as safe as we can make it, providing the security guards and rules that make sure neither the performers nor audience are harmed during shows.
Acts containing activities such as firebreathing or sword-swallowing are handled by trained professionals.

Q: How long does one have to go to clown school to become one full-time?

A: Most of our performers have only taken the one-year program before joining us. Some are even working with us as part of it!

Q: Do you do anything special for any holidays?

A: Yes we do! We have had christmas tale themed shows, halloween-decorated costumed shows, and firework cannon special shows during July (around the 4th of July).

Q: What is martin the clown doing now that he's retired?

A: He is still visiting some of our shows to be speaker, and still involved with aspects of the circus.
Good thing, too, because those are some pretty big shoes to fill!
